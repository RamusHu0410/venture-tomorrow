The Opportunity

Most young people in Markham don't see AI and tech entrepreneurship as their future. There's a growing gap between AI literacy and access to foundational education, mentorship, and real-world opportunities. We're here to close that gap.

What We Do

Workshops

Hands-on learning in AI, entrepreneurship, and innovation designed specifically for middle schoolers (Grades 5-8).

Competition

A showcase platform where students pitch ideas, build projects, and compete in a supportive environment.

Pipeline

A sustained pathway connecting curious students to mentors, industry professionals, and ongoing opportunities.

We're building the next generation of tech founders, creators, and leaders who understand that entrepreneurship and AI innovation can start right here in Markham.

Why You?

If you're someone who:

  • Believes in AI education as a critical skill for the future
  • Has entrepreneurial roots and wants to pass that forward
  • Has a genuine connection to Markham and sees the potential in our youth
  • Wants to build something meaningful alongside a team that gets it

Then you're exactly who we need on this journey.

What We Need From You

  • Join the team: Help us design workshops, mentor students, or lead initiatives.
  • Connect us with sponsors: Know companies or individuals invested in youth tech education?
  • Show up: Volunteer at the fall competition, attend info sessions, be visible and supportive.

No matter your role—founder, volunteer, sponsor, or ally—we have a place for you.
